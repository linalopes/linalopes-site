Interactive Installations, Bioart, and Wearable Technology

As an artist and technologist, my work explores the intersection of biological systems, digital environments, and human interaction. From interactive wearables to bio-art installations, I seek to merge the organic with the digital, reflecting on the future of art and humanity.

AI & Bioart

CryptoNudes

An exploration of machine learning, art, and human embodiment, challenging AI’s limitations in understanding the human body using a dataset from vintage photography.

Wearable Technology

CyBaby

A wearable neural helmet designed for newborns, capturing and visualizing brainwave data in real-time, offering a unique exploration of motherhood through art and technology.

Interactive Installation

Cellular Installation

An interactive installation using modular pieces and conductive thread, encouraging participants to expand the artwork through interaction, creating a living organism of light and texture.
